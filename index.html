    • Visit the Raydium website and connect your Phantom wallet.
    • Paste the Zumbu token address into Raydium.

    • When Phantom prompts you for a wallet signature, sign the transaction.
    • Paste the Zumbu token address into Raydium again, select Zumbu, and confirm.
    • Sign the wallet signature prompt from Phantom.

  4. ⁠Swap SOL for Zumbu Coin

    • On Raydium, select SOL in the “From” field and Zumbu in the “To” field.
    • Enter the amount of SOL you want to swap for Zumbu
    • Confirm the swap and approve the transaction in Phantom.
